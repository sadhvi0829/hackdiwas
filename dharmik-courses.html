About Dharmik

Dharmik is a renowned Hip Hop dancer and choreographer who has trained in multiple dance forms across the globe. With a background in modern dance, ballet, and jazz, he brings a unique fusion of styles to his teaching. His innovative approach combines traditional contemporary techniques with modern movement vocabulary.
