Explore Dance Forms

Kathak

North Indian classical dance form known for its intricate footwork and storytelling.

Bharatanatyam

South Indian classical dance form characterized by fixed upper torso and bent legs.

Telugu classical dance form known for its graceful movements and dramatic elements.

Odissi

East Indian classical dance form featuring fluid movements and sculpturesque poses.

Kerala's classical dance form known for its graceful, swaying movements.

Modern dance form combining elements of classical and contemporary styles.
