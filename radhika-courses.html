Module 1: Foundations of Kathak

4 Weeks

What You'll Learn:

  • Basic footwork (Tatkar)
  • Hand gestures (Mudras)
  • Body postures and positions
  • Introduction to rhythm (Taal)
  • Basic spins and turns

Resources Included:

  • Video tutorials
  • Practice music tracks
  • Written notes
  • Weekly assignments

6 Weeks

  • Practice sessions

  • Feedback sessions
